Coronavirus disease (COVID-19) advice for the public

Basic protective measures against the new coronavirus

Stay aware on the latest information on the COVID-19 outbreak, available on the WHO website and through your national and local public health authority. COVID -19 is still affecting mostly people in CHINA with with some outbreaks in other countries. Most people who become infected experience mild illness and recover, but it can be more severe for others.
